Our Mission & Focus

DSIL expands imaging science beyond traditional photography, applying it to AI imaging, VR, drones, medicine, forensics, and more.
We focus on future-oriented, practical research and nurture creative problem-solvers.

Our lab is dedicated to advancing the frontiers of imaging science. We believe in a hands-on, practical approach to research, tackling real-world problems and developing innovative solutions. We are committed to fostering a collaborative and stimulating environment where students and researchers can grow their skills and contribute to impactful projects. Our core research themes revolve around next-generation imaging technologies, intelligent image analysis, and their diverse applications.
